GAME REVIEW: POKEMON RANCH

Okay, let me say one thing.  This, "Device", not, "game" is awesome.  This thing hardly qualifys as a game, yet i love it.  Here are the features:

1. import Pokemon from Diamond/Pearl.
2. Raise them in a Ranch.
3. Play as your Mii.

......I just can't wait to play as myself (my mii) raising Pokemon in a Ranch, yet, why call this a game? So.............

Rating: 9.5/10 For: Awesome features, yet it doesn't really qualify as a game, even though it's considered one......
