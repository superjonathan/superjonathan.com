exDragon: Get Pokemon like Salamence, Altaria, & Rayquaza!

exDeoxys: Get Pokemon like Deoxys & Rayquaza!

exEmerald: After that time in Kanto again, we go back to the new Pokemon of Hoenn with Pokemon like Manectric & Milotic!

exUnseen Forces: We go back to another region yet again, this time in Johto, to see Pokemon like Ho-oh & Lugia!

exDelta Species: Get Pokemon like Metagross!

exLegend Maker: Get Pokemon like Arcanine & Mew!

exHolon Phantoms: Get Pokemon like Mewtwo!
